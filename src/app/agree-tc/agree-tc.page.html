If a Material Change occurs during the commitment period, Vairt will disclose prominently on its website details of the material change; notify committed investors of the material change and require them to reconfirm their commitment within 5 business days; and if reconfirmation is not provided within 5 business days, cancel the commitment.

The commitment period is to be the period from your investment in the property on the Website until the transaction is completed, meaning the property has been transferred from Seller to the Investors

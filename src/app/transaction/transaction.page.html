The polling will be conducted over a set period of time.

During this time you will be able to raise questions seeking clarifications.

All investors of the relevant property will be required to cast their vote.

At the conclusion of the voting period, we will poll the voting and communicate the decisions to all investors.

We would require at least 20% of the investors to cast their vote for the vote to be valid
